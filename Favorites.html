 S Rank - These are a few of the games I would personally consider in the list for best of all time. 
  1. Final Fantasy 14 - Final Fantasy 14 is one of many entries into the Final Fantasy franchise. Unlike many of its predecessors however it is an MMO, or a Massively Multiplayer Online game. It has an absolutely amazing, award winning storyline that, while may be slow at first, picks up fast and kept me hooked onto it for ages just waiting for the next update anxious to see what else it has to offer

  2. Okami - Okami is a very particular kind of game. It has a gorgeous traditional japanese artstyle and most of the mechanics in the game revolve around using an ink brush to perform various techniques as you roam the world of Japan as the goddess of the sun. While there may be a lot of motionblur that may put some people off, the game still has much to offer. It has 3 seperate and unique storylines you play through that all come together as one cohesive story. Having been released in 2006 some of it may show its age a littl bit, but with its unique style and gameplay it still feels very new and fresh, especially with it's HD remasters that came later.

  3. Super Smash Bros - The Super Smash Bros. series has been around for a decently long time at this point, having it's first game release for the Nintendo 64 at the start of 1999. Since then, this fighting game has added many new characters to its roster and added many new features. Though some may argue which of the games in the series is best, whether it be the long time favorite of Melee or the newer addition Ultimate, and whether you play competitively or just casually, you'd be hardpressed to find a gamer who doesn't enjoy at least one game in the series.
